About Us

Making a Video Shouldn't be in Vyond Studio. It Should Be in the LVM.

BluePeacocks's mission is to enable GoAnimaters to make videos — quickly and easily.

Since the LVM shut down in 2019, and original GoAnimate For Schools shut down on 30th June 2019, people tried to use the LVM, to continue their GoAnimate career.

What's the problem? Trying to get the LVM back. People tried 000webhost pages, (i owned BluePeacocks Images/ tvy4gv in september 2019 and in early 2020), and they all went to sleep. But then February '20 came. They disovered GoAnimate Remastered, but back on March 2nd '20, it got DMCA'd. so, how to get it?


Uncomplexify!

That's how GA4SR started. Back around the end of February '20, BluePeacocks wanted to make a LVM copy, then looked at GoAnimate Remastered, and thought, “I really want GoAnimate For Schools back. I'm going to need to do something about that.”

Being of an entrepreneurial bent, he built out a team to develop the platform. 15 days later, they made it. And it worked. Point, click, drag, drop. Voila! Animated videos for everyone.

BluePeacocks the Creator, started GA4SR in February '20.
